# Investor & Client Demo Script

## The 30-Second Pitch
"Aklak is a localized, Arabic-first food delivery platform designed specifically for the Egyptian and Saudi markets. Unlike generic white-label solutions, Aklak is built from the ground up as a multi-role ecosystem. We don't just have a customer app; we have an interconnected suite featuring the Customer App, Restaurant Dashboard, Driver App, and a powerful Admin Control Panel. What you are about to see is our interactive Prototype V1, which demonstrates the full lifecycle of an order happening in real-time."

## The 2-Minute Demo Script
1. **Open the Showcase Page:** "Here is our launchpad. You can see the four pillars of our platform."
2. **Click 'Login as Customer':** "The customer experience is mobile-first, clean, and entirely in Arabic. Notice the localized currency and UX."
3. **Add to Cart & Checkout:** "We've gamified the experience with promo codes and loyalty points. I'll place an order now."
4. **Switch to Restaurant:** "Instantly, the restaurant receives the order. They accept it, prepare it, and mark it ready."
5. **Switch to Driver:** "The driver receives the ping, picks up the food, and marks it delivered. The money hits their digital wallet."
6. **Switch to Admin:** "Finally, as the platform owners, we see the bird's-eye view. Total revenue, active drivers, and live orders."

## The 5-Minute Walkthrough
*(Follow the 2-minute script, but add these deep dives)*
- **Deep Dive - Customer:** Show the restaurant search, category filters, and the "AKLAK10" promo code application. Mention the points system.
- **Deep Dive - Restaurant:** Show the Menu management and Offers section. Explain how restaurants have full control over their storefront.
- **Deep Dive - Driver:** Show the Earnings history. Explain how transparency helps retain drivers.
- **Deep Dive - Admin:** Show the Finance tab and system settings. Emphasize that the platform is built to scale across multiple countries.

## Key Selling Points
- **Arabic-First UX:** Not an afterthought translation.
- **Multi-Role Architecture:** Solves the classic "chicken-and-egg" marketplace problem by providing tools for all stakeholders.
- **Scalable Foundation:** The frontend is decoupled, ready to be plugged into our Supabase/PostgreSQL backend infrastructure.

## Current Limitations
- Data is currently isolated to the browser (LocalStorage) to ensure the demo is always blazing fast and never goes down during a pitch.
- Maps and payment gateways are mocked for visual representation.

## Next Roadmap (Backend Integration Phase)
- **Phase 1:** Supabase PostgreSQL Database integration (Tables already structured).
- **Phase 2:** Real Authentication (Supabase Auth built, pending activation).
- **Phase 3:** Third-party integrations (Stripe/Paymob for payments, Google Maps for tracking).
